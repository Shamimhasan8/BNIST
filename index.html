About BNIST

Bahaul Huq NPI Institute of Science and Technology stands as a beacon of technical education excellence in Bangladesh.

Shaping Tomorrow's Engineers

Located in the heart of Narayanganj, BNIST has been at the forefront of technical education, providing comprehensive programs in Civil, Computer, Electrical, Mechanical, and Textile Technology. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every graduate is equipped with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills essential for today's competitive job market.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and industry partnerships, we create an environment where innovation thrives and students develop into competent professionals ready to contribute to Bangladesh's technological advancement.

Our Mission

To provide world-class technical education that bridges theory with practical application, preparing students for successful careers in engineering and technology.

Our Vision

To be a leading institute of technical education, recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and innovation in science and technology.

Excellence

Committed to maintaining the highest standards in education, faculty expertise, and student achievement across all our technology programs.

Community

Building a supportive learning environment that fosters collaboration, creativity, and professional growth among students and faculty.

Our Programs

Choose from our comprehensive range of technology programs designed to prepare you for a successful career in your chosen field.

Civil Technology

4 Years Diploma

Master the fundamentals of construction, structural design, and infrastructure development. Learn modern techniques in building design, materials science, and project management.

Key Areas:

  • Structural Design
  • Construction Management
  • Surveying
  • Materials Testing

Computer Technology

4 Years Diploma

Dive into the world of programming, software development, and digital technologies. Gain expertise in modern computing systems and emerging technologies.

Key Areas:

  • Programming Languages
  • Database Systems
  • Web Development
  • Network Administration

Electrical Technology

4 Years Diploma

Explore electrical systems, power generation, and electronic circuits. Develop skills in power systems, automation, and renewable energy technologies.

Key Areas:

  • Power Systems
  • Electronic Circuits
  • Automation
  • Renewable Energy

Mechanical Technology

4 Years Diploma

Learn about machines, manufacturing processes, and mechanical systems. Focus on design, maintenance, and optimization of mechanical equipment.

Key Areas:

  • Machine Design
  • Manufacturing
  • Thermodynamics
  • Quality Control

Textile Technology

4 Years Diploma

Understand textile production, fabric design, and garment manufacturing. Gain expertise in modern textile machinery and quality assurance.

Key Areas:

  • Fabric Production
  • Garment Design
  • Quality Assurance
  • Textile Machinery
